
['Live Pangolin Rescued In Berhampur\nBerhampur: A live pangolin was rescued from Binayak Nagar in Laxmi Nrusingha Sahi under Bada Bazar police limits in Berhampur on Tuesday. Reportedly, locals had spotted the pangolin crossing the road. On getting information, forest department officials rushed to the area and rescued the pangolin.']
