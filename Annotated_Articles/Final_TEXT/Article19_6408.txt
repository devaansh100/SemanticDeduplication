
['Four arrested for poaching leopard, two others at large\nHyderabad: Five persons who allegedly killed a leopard by electrocuting it in November were arrested by forest officials in Adilabad district on Wednesday.Forest officials said that the accused, Chavan Namdev, Chavan Krishna, Sidam Nago Rao and P Nagender, were arrested by the enforcement officials of the Forest Department. However, two more accused are still at large.When the leopard was killed by the accused on November 10, the officials were unaware of the development. On Tuesday, after receiving a tip-off, officials went to Umarda village and nabbed Namdev, after which the details of the offence came to light. Officials recovered the leopard’s claws from the possession of the accused.“During interrogation, Namdev said that a live wire was laid to poach the wild animals in a sorghum field. He disclosed that he was assisted by six other persons in the offence,” forest officials said.To evade arrest, the accused burnt the carcass of the animal after collecting nine of its claws, a tooth and seven whiskers.Forest officials said that they are investigating to find out whether the accused had committed any similar offences in the past and said that they have launched a manhunt for the two absconding accused.']
