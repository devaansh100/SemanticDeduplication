
['Maharashtra: Woman farmer killed by tiger in Mul\nCHANDRAPUR: Third human kill in wild animal attacks was reported from Chandrapur district within a span of less than 36 hours. A woman farmer working in her farm at Kosambi village in Mul taluka was killed by a tiger on Friday evening.This is fifth human kill this year in predator attacks.Deceased Dnyaneshwari Vasudev Mohurle (55) was working in her field when a tiger killed her and dragged the body to nearly 50 meters. As soon as villagers came to know about the incident, they rushed to the spot.Police and foresters also reached the spot and completed inquest formalities. The forest department has provided ex gratia assistance of Rs30,000 to the relatives of the deceased.']
