Pic/Istock On Saturday a tiger was found dead with injury marks near a railway track in Gondia district of Maharashtra, stated an official from the forest department. A railway trackman spotted the carcass near the tracks between Vadegaon and Wadsa after which the forest department was alerted. "The area where the big cat was found falls in Korambhi beat of Arjuni Morgaon forest range," said assistant conservator of forest (ACF) Dada Raut.
