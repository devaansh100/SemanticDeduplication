
["Wanted rhino poacher arrested in Kerala\nGUWAHATI: Assam Police on Thursday arrested a wanted rhino poacher and the main perpetrator of the poaching incident at Kaziranga National Park on January 20 from Kerala 's Malappuram district with the help of the local police.The accused has been identified as Asmat Ali alias Nalco (35) of Milonpur village in Assam's Biswanath district. He was arrested from Nilambur.On February 2, Assam Police had announced a cash reward of Rs 2 lakh against Ali and Rs 2 lakh against two others, Ataur Rahman alias Bassa (35) and Abdul Matin alias Kala (36). A day after the announcement, police arrested a total of five rhino poachers in connection with the poaching case and recovered a suspected rhino horn from their possession. They were identified as Aslam Sheikh, Hamid Sheikh, Ghanashyam Gupta, Mantu Chamuah and Jamir Ahmed.A carcass of a female rhino was recovered with a gunshot wound and horn missing in the Kohora range of the park on January 20. It was the first poaching case of the year. Following this, police in Golaghat arrested a poacher who revealed the involvement of Asmat Ali alias Nalco, Ataur Rahman alias Bassa and Abdul Matin alias Kala of Biswanath district.Nilambur police station officer P Vishnu said Ali had sneaked into Kerala after the Assam Police issued a lookout notice against him.A total of 54 rhinos were killed in Kaziranga in 2013 and 2014, which was the highest in the last 21 years. However, the trend gradually decreased. Seventeen poaching cases were reported in 2015, 18 in 2016, six in 2017, seven in 2018, three in 2019, two in 2020 and one last year."]
