Police arrest 4, seize tiger skin
Police arrest 4, seize tiger skin Thane, Apr 21 (UNI) The city police on Tuesday arrested four persons all from Mumbai and seized from their possession stripped (black and yellow) tiger skin and also a paw containing five nails the police said.  Sr. PI Ganpat Pingle of the Kongaon police station of Bhiwandi zone told this correspondent that acting on a tip off that some persons animal poachers were to come to Bhiwandi to sell the stipped tiger skin the police laid a trap.
