In a well-coordinated wildlife operation carried out by joint team of #WCCB and H.P Police on 20-07-2021, various wildlife contrabands including 02 leopard #skins, 14 leopard #claws, and 12 leopard #teeth including canines were seized from Himachal Pradesh.
