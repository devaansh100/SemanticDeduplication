Boat with 500 kg sea cucumber heading towards Rameswaram intercepted, one held
Ramanathapuram (Tamil Nadu) [India], July 11 (ANI): The Mandapam Forest Department of Tamil Nadu's Ramanathapuram district intercepted a country boat heading towards Rameswaram with 500 kg of sea cucumber mid-sea. The department has seized the boat and has arrested the accused in the case. An investigation is underway at the Mandapam forest office. (ANI)
