
['Two Poachers Arrested In Jajpur\nJajpur: Acting on a tip-off, officials of Bari forest department arrested two poachers under Dalijoda forest range in Jajpur district of Odisha on Sunday. The poachers were held while they were on their way to a forest for poaching. The officials have also seized a country-made gun, bullets and a motorbike from their possession. “Further investigation into the matter is on,” said officer-in-charge of Bari forest department.']
