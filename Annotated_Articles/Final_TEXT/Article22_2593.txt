
['Female Elephant Electrocuted To Death In Bargarh\nBargarh: A female elephant has been electrocuted to death near Goudapali road at Alekhpur village under Ambabhona block in Bargarh district on Monday. Reportedly, the jumbo died after coming in contact with a live wire. The age of the female elephant is estimated to be around 15 to 16 years. A herd of elephants entered the village from the Barapahada forest. One among them came in contact with a live electric wire lost her life. On getting information, the forest officials and the Hirakud Wildlife Division arrived at the scene and have initiated a probe.']
