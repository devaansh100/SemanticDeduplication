
["Andhra Pradesh: Task force arrests two smugglers, seizes red sanders logs worth Rs 50 lakh\nTIRUPATI: The Red Sanders Anti-Smuggling Task Force (RSASTF) arrested two men and seized 42 logs of red sanders wood worth Rs 50 lakh from their possession from two different places in Andhra Pradehs's Chittoor district on Wednesday.Acting on a tip-off, superintendent of police Meda Sundar Rao sent two teams to KVB Puram mandal on Tuesday night on a combing mission.The two teams launched their combing operations from Vallur and Narayanavanam reserve forest areas, where they spotted some workers carrying wooden logs on their shoulders.One Polaiah (52) of Valluru was caught while the others fled the spot. The cops recovered 16 wooden logs from the spot. A police case was registered in this regard.The other team also found a group of men carrying the wooden logs and asked them to stop.However, the men did not pay heed to the warning by the cops to surrender and started running.The policemen chased and caught one Venkataiah alias Pottodu (37) from the spot.“Murali, Vinay, Uday of Valluru and Subbulu, Chiranjeevi and Venkataiah of Pathapalem were among those who fled from the spot,” said a police officer."]
