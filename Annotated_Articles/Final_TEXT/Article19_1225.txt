Sources in MP forest department suspect the tiger was poisoned. The animal, aged 4 to 5 years, was photographed near Lunavada in the first week of this month. Despite confirming its presence, the department failed to protect the tiger believed to have lived in and around Kantar village for over six months. The decaying carcass was found by the forest department 7 km from where the animal was first spotted by a school teacher. A post-mortem is being carried out as per protocol, confirmed sources. The animal is believed to have walked into Gujarat around a year ago, but an official confirmation had come in from the forest department on February 14.']
