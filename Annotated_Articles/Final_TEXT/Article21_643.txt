
['Tirupati: Kadapa police arrest 13 red sanders smugglers, seize 22 red sanders logs\nTIRUPATI: Kadapa district police wing which conducted raids at multiple locations across the district arrested 13 red sanders smugglers and seized 22 red sanders logs on Friday.In the first raid carried out near Rani Baavi along the Mydukur-Badvel highway, police arrested five smugglers and seized 10 logs along with a four-wheeler and a two-wheeler.In the second raid carried out at Gangayapalli, the Kalasapadu police arrested six smugglers and seized 8 logs along with a mobile phone.Meanwhile, Kodur police carried out raids near the Reddemma Podu vanka and arrested two smugglers and seized 4 red sanders logs from the possession of the accused.Kadapa SP KKN Anburajan appreciated the efforts of the different police units in the arrests and added that the preventive detention (PD) Act will be invoked on the smugglers.']
