Police arrest three persons with leopard head KATHMANDU: Police have arrested three persons with a leopard head from Kathmandu Mall at Sundhara, Kathmandu. Acting on a tip-off, a special police team deployed from Metropolitan Crime Branch, arrested Santosh Ale Magar, 23, of Dudhpokhara rural municipality-6, Lamjung, Sujan Thing, 20, from Rosi Ruralmunicipality-12, Kavrepalanchowk and Mingmar Moktan, 18, from Rosi Ruralmunicipality-12, Kavrepalanchowk. They have been sent to Division Forest Office, Kathmandu for further action.
