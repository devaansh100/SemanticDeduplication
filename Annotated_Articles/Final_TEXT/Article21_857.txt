
['Man tries to sell fake tiger skin, held in Sangareddy\nMan tries to sell fake tiger skin, held in Sangareddy By Telangana Today Published: Updated On - 12:53 AM, Wed - 17 November 21 Sangareddy: A man who attempted to sell fake tiger skin was arrested by Patancheru Police. As he was carrying the skin on his bike, the locals, who observed him moving suspiciously, informed the Patancheru Police. Sub-inspector of Police B Sailu took the accused Mohammad Kudrat (37), a resident of Saidabad in Hyderabad, into custody. The fake skin and his bike were recovered from the Kudrath.
