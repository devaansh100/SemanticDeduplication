Two poachers arrested for killing antelope in Nashik
Two poachers arrested for killing antelope in Nashik Nashik, Oct 11 (UNI) The Forest department squad has arrested two poachers for allegedly hunting a male antelope in Nandgaon forest reserve of the district. While patrolling on Saturday night at around 0100 hours in Jamdari to Panjan road, a squad of Nandgaon forest range found two people riding suspiciously on a two-wheeler. When the squad tried to stop the poachers, the duo tried to flee away on the vehicle, but the squad gave a chase and arrested them.
