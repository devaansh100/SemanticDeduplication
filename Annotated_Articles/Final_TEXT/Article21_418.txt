
['Leopard Skin Seized In Odisha’s Balangir, One Held\nBalangir: Forest officials seized a Leopard skin from a village in Odisha’s Balangir district late on Monday and arrested a person. Acting on a tip-off, a team of Forest officials of Saintala range raided the house of Biswamitra Rana of Kansar village and seized a Leopard skin measuring 2.6 inches and weighing 2 kg from his possession. The interrogation of the accused is underway to ascertain the involvement of more people in this case.']
