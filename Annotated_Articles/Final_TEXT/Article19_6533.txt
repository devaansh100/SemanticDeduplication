
['Tribal man killed by tiger in Wayanad\nWAYANAD: An elderly tribal man was killed in a tiger attack at Wayanad wildlife sanctuary. The deceased has been identified as Jadayan, a native of the Pachadi colony in Vadakkanadu of the Kurichya range. According to sources, Jadayan has gone missing ever since he went to the forest to collect wood. It was the forest guards who found the half-eaten dead body in the forest.']
