
['STF apprehends a wildlife criminal, a live pangolin seized from his possession\nBhubaneswar, June 8 (UNI) A team of the Special Task Force (STF) has apprehended a wildlife criminal from Boudh district and seized a live pangolin weighing 12. 4 Kg and other incriminating materials from his possession. STF sources here on Wednesday said on the basis of reliable information, a team of STF conducted raid with the help of Boudh Forest Officials on Tuesday near Khuntigura village in between Kantamal-Baliguda Road under Kantamala Police station of Boudh district and apprehended one Biseswar Bagarti of Tentuliamand under Tarava Police station of Sonepur District.']
