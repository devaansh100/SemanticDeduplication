Teak Wood Logs Worth Rs 1L Seized In Cuttack
Cuttack: The forest department personnel have seized a huge cache of Teak wood logs from a pickup van at Manpur village In Cuttack. The estimated value of the wood logs is around Rs 1 lakh, sources informed. Acting on a tip-off, forest officials conducted a raid at Banki-Baideshiv road and seized around 30 wood logs from the vehicle. However, the forest department failed to arrest the wood mafia in this connection.
