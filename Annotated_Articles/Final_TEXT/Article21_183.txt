
['4 held for trying to sell ‘tiger skin’\n4 held for trying to sell ‘tiger skin’ Home Ahmedabad Crime 4 held for trying to sell ‘tiger skin’ 1 / 2 2 / 2  Acting on a tip-off, Crime Branch arrest trio from near old A’bad fire HQ; reach fourth accused after questioning them Acting on a tip-off, Crime Branch arrest trio from near old A’bad fire HQ; reach fourth accused after questioning them  The Ahmedabad Crime Branch arrested three persons for attempting to sell what looked like tiger skin in the open ground at the old Ahmedabad fire headquarters on Wednesday.  Nailesh Jani, 55, resident of Gollimda, Ranchod Babubhai Prajapati (40) resident of Harkishandas Sheth ni pol, near Mandvi ni Pol, Aastodia and Alpesh Dholakia, 40, resident of Mandvi ni Pol, were arrested by the crime branch team under supervision of PI A Y Baloch. A tip-off received by the crime branch was that the criminals were present at the old fire headquarters land to sell tiger and other animal skin.  The team recovered four skins of an animal resembling a tiger worth Rs 4,00,000, a two-wheeler worth Rs 40,000 and three mobile phones worth Rs 18,000 were seized from the three. The police learnt during questioning Jani that one Mohan Rathod (46), resident of Gulbai Tekra, had given Jani the four full skins to sell them off about two years back.  Rathod was arrested by the police on Thursday and he is being questioned on how he procured the same.  ACP Crime Branch D P Chudasama said, “We have registered a case against the four under the Wild Life Protection Act and are questioning them further.”  ~~81578413~~']
