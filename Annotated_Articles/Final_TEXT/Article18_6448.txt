
['STF Seizes Two Elephant Tusks In Keonjhar, One Held\nSTF Seizes Two Elephant Tusks In Keonjhar, One Held Keonjhar: The Special Task Force (STF) of the Odisha Crime Branch on Saturday seized two elephant tusks in Karanjia and arrested a person in this connection. The arrestee has been identified as Ramakant Pradhan is a native of Kalanda village in Parajangpur under Keonjhar police limits, is suspected to be a poacher or a trader, the STF said. Acting on a tip-off, the officials conducted a raid at a spot between Patung and Kalanda villages in Keonjhar Sadar on Saturday night and detained a Pradhan in this connection. The cops also seized two elephant tusks weighing 5.66 Kg and incriminating materials from his possession. Further investigation is underway to trace the other accused involved in the illegal trade, police said.']
