The team deputed from Police Station Koteswor held Raj Deula, 25, and Rahul Deula, 20, who have been residing in Kirtipur municipality-9, with 10 tortoises on Sunday evening.  Spokesperson at District Police Range Kathmandu Dinesh Raj Mainali informed that the police team arrested both from a caf√© at Kathmandu metropolis-32, Koteswor on charge of being involved in illegal trafficking of wildlife.  Police recovered 10 tortoises, three sets of mobile and a motorbike (Ba 99 Pa 4944) from the smugglers. Mainali further shared that the Koteswor police station is carrying out investigation into the incident.']
