
["MP: Female tiger cub found dead in Bandhavgarh reserve\nA female tiger was found dead in Madhya Pradesh's Bandhavgarh reserve on Monday. By Press Trust of India: A female tiger cub was found dead in Madhya Pradesh’s Bandhavgarh Tiger Reserve (BTR), a forest official said on Monday. Some forest beat guards on Sunday spotted the carcass, having serious injury marks on head, stomach, back and ears, in Dhamokhar buffer zone near Barbaspur area of the reserve, BTR’s field director B S Annigeri said. advertisement It is suspected that the cub, which was 7-8 months old, was killed in an attack by a male tiger, the official said. According to the All-India Tiger Estimation Report 2018, Madhya Pradesh was home to 526 tigers, the highest in any state of the country. MP has a number of tiger reserves, including Kanha, Bandhavgarh, Pench, Satpura and Panna. --- ENDS ---"]
