
['Tamil Nadu: Hunt on for leopard that killed five goats\nERODE: Four forest department teams, along with a team of police, on Sunday launched a search for a leopard that killed five goats in a village near Sathyamangalam Tiger Reserve (STR).A forest officer said the animal came out from STR in the early hours of the day and entered Pondimokkai village near Bannari . "The leopard then killed five goats that were reared by 45-year-old U Murugan ."On Saturday night, the officer said, Murugan left all the five goats in a pen after grazing them. "They were kept tied in the pen. He woke up around 6am on Sunday and found all the goats were attacked and killed by some wild animal."Murugan alerted the forest department and a team of officers rushed to the spot. "We have found pug marks of the leopard in the locality. We have collected samples of the same and sent them for verification. We will set up a trap for the animal," the officer said. He said five teams - four of them from the forest department and one from Pikkiripalayam police beat - were combing the nearby forest areas to track the leopard.']
