
["Elephant tusk seized in Odisha, 1 held\nA man has been arrested from Odisha's Angul district for allegedly being involved in wildlife crime and an elephant tusk was seized from his possession, police said on Wednesday. Acting on a tip-off, a team of the special task force personnel of the state police on Tuesday conducted a search operation in an area near Antulia under the Angul Forest Division and apprehended the person, a senior officer said.
