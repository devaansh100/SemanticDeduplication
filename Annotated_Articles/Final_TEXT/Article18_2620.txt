
['Bsf Man From Palghar Dies Of Snake Bite\nMUMBAI: A Border Security Force ( BSF ) jawan posted at Madhopur in Pathankot district of Punjab died of a snake bite while sleeping outside his house on Sunday. Mahesh Fadwale (32) was cremated with full military honours at his ancestral home in Kahre-Talavali village in Jawhar taluka of Palghar on Monday.']
