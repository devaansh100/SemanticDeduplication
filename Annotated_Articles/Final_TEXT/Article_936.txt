Inter-state pangolin smuggling racket busted in Sambalpur, 7 arrested
27/05/2020 at 8:41 AM Sambalpur, May 27: Rairakhol forest officials on Tuesday busted an inter-state pangolin smuggling racket and arrested seven persons, including a former sarpanch. The arrested have been identified as Sushil Sethi, Laxman Sethi, Shriram Sabar, Shir Sabar, Khiju Sabar, Jadumani Patra and Nepal Sahu. Pangolin scales weighing 2.6 kg have been seized from their possession. The arrested were trading pangolin and their scales in and outside the state.
