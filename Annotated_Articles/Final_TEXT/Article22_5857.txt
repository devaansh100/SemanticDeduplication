
["Leopard enters residential area and attacks dog in Maharashtra's Nashik, clip goes viral\nLeopard enters residential area and attacks dog in Maharashtra's Nashik, clip goes viral A clip caught by a CCTV camera outside a home in Nashik shows the distressing moment a black dog was attacked by a leopard. The short clip shows the canine resting atop a small wall when the big cat enters the compound and starts chasing it around. The poor dog runs in all directions but the leopard manages to get hold. The nail-biting clip ends with the predator walking away with the dog in its mouth. According to news agency ANI, the incident took place in Mungsare village of Nashik on June 5. Forest department officials told locals in the area were asked to remain alert. They reportedly added traps in the area to catch the predator. “We appeal to the people of Mungsare village to remain indoors at night as leopard activity has increased in this area,” Pankaj Garg, Deputy Conservator of Forest, Nashik told ANI.Credit: Twitter/ANI"]
