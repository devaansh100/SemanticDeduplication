
['Illicit timber seized in Bandipora: Police\nSrinagar: Police seized illicit timber from Aloosa area of Bandipora on Saturday. A statement of J&K Police issued here said that acting on a specific information about timber smuggling, Police raided the particular spot and seized nine logs of illicit timber dumped in the cowshed belonging to MakhnaChechi son of ChechinarAloosa who is presently at large and efforts are on to effect his arrest.']
