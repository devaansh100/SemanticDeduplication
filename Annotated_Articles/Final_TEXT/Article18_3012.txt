According to the forest official, the incident, which occurred at Gothane village in Ratnagiri district, came to light days after the four accused were booked for illegally entering Chandoli National Park, which is part of the reserve, with one of them carrying a gun for hunting. The accused were being probed after they were booked on March 31 for illegally entering the forest when the crime came to light. The accused were identified as Sandeep Tukaram Pawar, Mangesh Kamtekar, Akshay Kamtekar, and Ramesh Ghag. It is important to note that the Bengal monitor lizard is a reserved species under the Wildlife Protection Act 1972.']
