
['Watch: Elephant tramples woman to death in Odisha, herd attacks villagers\nJun 14, 2022, 05:17PM IST Source: TOI.in A bizarre incident came to light when an elephant trampled a woman to death at Raipal village in Odisha’s Dhenkenal district on June 9. The elephant attacked a 68-year-old, Maya Murmu when she had gone to fetch water. According to the sources, Murmu saw an elephant herd coming in her direction. When she tried to flee, one of the elephants chased and trampled her. Another attack by a herd of elephants was made when she was being taken for cremation by villagers the same evening. Villagers fled the spot leaving Murmu’s body, which one of the jumbos flung away. The herd damaged her house and those of her neighbours. Watch the story to know more.']
