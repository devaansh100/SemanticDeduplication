
['Dead leopard found in Odisha’s Forest\nODISHA: The forest officials found an adult leopard’s body in Chhendipada forest range of Odisha. According to the report of TOI, the leopard’s carcass was found inside the Kanloi Reserve. The forest officials have sent the leopard’s body for autopsy. The officials are trying to ascertain the reason behind the leopard’s death.']
