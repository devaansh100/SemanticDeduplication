
['Odisha Forest Department arrests 2 poachers\nBhubaneswar: The Odisha Forest Department has arrested two poachers from near Chandanchaturi on behalf of the Pithabata Wildlife Range under the Simlipal Tiger Conservation Project (South). Reportedly, both the poachers were captured by a staff led by Ranger Jyotiranjan Senapati while entering into Simlipal for hunting. The officials have seized firearms, ammunition and gun powder from their possession and have forwarded them to the court. An investigation has been launched after Ranger Senapati made another complaint about possession of illegal firearms and explosives at Badasahi police station.']
