Illegal ivory trade in Kochi: 5 arrested
Kochi: Five persons were arrested from here over illegal trade of ivories. The arrest was made at an apartment in Tripunithura by the forest officials after a tip-off. Tripunithura native Roshan, Ernakulam native Shebin Sasi, Iringalakkuda native Midhun, Paravoor natives Sanoj and Shemeer were taken in custody by the officials of Kodanad Forest Range and Ernakulam Forest Flying Squad. Along with the ivories, an ivory sculpture was also seized from them. Apart from these, two knives used for ivory carving, other equipment and cash counting machine were also taken in custody. The ivories seized is worth around Rs 45 lakh, said the officials. They also informed that case has been registered under Wildlife Protection Act.
