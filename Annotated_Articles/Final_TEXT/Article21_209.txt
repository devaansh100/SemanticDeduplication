
['Forest dept seizes 18 tiger nails; 2 teeth\nForest Mobile Squad arrested two persons who were trying to sell tiger nails and teeth at Hunsur, in the district, on Thursday. The authorities took Shankar, 40, of K R Pet taluk in Mandya district and Ganesh, 48, of Hunsur, are the accused who were smuggling 18 tiger nails and two teeth, said Deputy Conservator of Forests A T Poovaiah.']
