
['6-year-old killed in leopard attack in village near Nashik\nNashik: A six-year-old girl was killed in a leopard attack in the Dhondegaon village, near Nashik city , on Wednesday night.At 8.30 pm, the girl was playing outside her home when a leopard pounced on her. Only after her relatives and the people nearby started shouting, the leopard left the girl and fled.The girl died on the spot as she had received serious injuries, said a forest official.The incident has sparked panic among the residents in the area. The villagers have demanded thats cages be put up immediately to trap the leopard.Forest officials said they conducted patrolling in the nearby areas in the morning, and the cages are to be installed to trap the animal.Recently, another person was killed in a leopard attack in a village in Igatpuri taluka of Nashik district. Also, there have been three leopard attack incidents in various parts of the district where the victims could escape and survive.On April 10, a 25-year-old farm worker was injured in a leopard attack near Girnare village, around 20 km from Nashik city.Earlier this month, an eight-year-old girl was also injured in a leopard attack in the early morning hours near Girnare village.']
