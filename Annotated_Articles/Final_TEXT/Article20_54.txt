When the accused arrived and tried to shift the red sanders, the police swung into action and managed to nab thered handed. The police have recovered 117 kilograms ofworth around Rs 4.68 lakhs along with vehicles (car and a bike) worth Rs 5 lakh.The arrests were made on Sunday in the afternoon, between 2.15 to 2.45 pm.The arrested are identified as Mohammed Ismail, 55, of Hosakote, Vijay Aggi Ramayya, 36, N Penchalayya, 26, V Chandra, 42, all from Rasappli of Kadapa in Andhra Pradesh and Syyed Afroz, 43, of Somasundra in Hosakote.The team of policemen attached to Anugondanahalli police near Whitefield area received the tip off about the smugglers operating in outskirts of the city.“Three people got out of the car, waked to the, had a short discussion and then began handing over the red sanders from the car to the men on the bike.We immediately surrounded them and after inspecting the car, we saw logs of red sanders.
