
['Himalayan Bear Dies In Nandankanan Zoo\nBhubaneswar: A Himalayan Bear died at the Nandankanan Zoological Park in the outskirts of Bhubaneswar on Tuesday. As per reports, the Himalayan Bear named Akash was procured under the process of animal exchange. The bear was under veterinary care as it was sick for over one week. However, it died despite all efforts of the caretakers.']
