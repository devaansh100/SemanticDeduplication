
['Three held with skin, bones of leopard in Dindori district\nThree held with skin, bones of leopard in Dindori district   Staff Reporter : According to WCCB Jabalpur, during a special campaign against poaching and illegal trade of wildlife under the direction of In-charge officer of STF (Wildlife), Madhya Pradesh and Abhijit Roy Choudhary, Regional Deputy Director, WCCB Jabalpur, they received a tip-off that some persons were trying to sell leopard’s skin and bones.
