Three poachers arrested
MYSURU: Bandipur Tiger Reserve forest staff on Tuesday arrested three persons on charges of poaching deer.The arrested are Menasinakai Mahesh , Marimoole Mahesh, and Shivakumar The trio had hunted a deer at the Gopalaswamy Betta forest area coming under Bandipur Tiger Reserve. The foresters who got the tip-off arrested them and seized the carcass of the deer, a gun, torchlight , weapons, three bikes and two mobile phones.Bandipur ACF K Paramesh said the accused were involved in poaching since 2014 and foresters were searching for them.
