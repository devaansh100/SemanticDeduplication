
['Six fined for keeping deer meat\nCoimbatore: The forest department imposed a fine of 90,000 on six farm workers for keeping meat of a dead spotted deer at Jambukandi area near Anaikatti on Saturday.The male spotted deer stuck in a fence erected around a piece of farmland in the Periyanaickenpalayam forest range, when trying to escape from a stray dog. The dog killed the animal and starting eating it. Seeing this, six farmhands working on the field chased away the dog and shared meat among themselves. Forest officials got a tip about the matter and rushed to the area, where they caught six farm workers with meat. They seized meat from the workers and imposed a fine of 15,000 on each person. The workers are: S Murugan, 48, his wife M Sivakami , 38, M Shanthi, 37, S Valli , 29, S Sindhu , 28 and S Sivakami , 48. tnn']
