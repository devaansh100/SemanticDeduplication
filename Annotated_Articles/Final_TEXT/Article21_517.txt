
['Five red sanders smugglers held in Andhra Pradesh\nTIRUPATI: Mydukur police arrested a group of five red sanders smugglers and seized 20 red sanders logs weighing 446 kilos from the accused near the forest area at Nagapatnam village in Kadapa district on Saturday.The accused were identified as M Allurayya (25), M Rosaiah (24), K Sriramulu (48), N Tirupati (25) and B Madhu (26).']
