Dhenkanal: Five deer skin and two guns has been seized by Dhenkanal forest department and three people have been arrested in this connection. This recovery was done from Padhanatangara village under Kapilasa range limits in Dhenkanal district of Odisha. The forest department officials recovered deer and antler skins from a poacher named Ashok Nath of Gadia area. Two other poachers were also arrested in this connection for keeping the guns they used to hunt from Kamakhya Kapileswar and Pirabandra area. The Police has forwarded the three arrested persons to court, informed the forest department officials.
