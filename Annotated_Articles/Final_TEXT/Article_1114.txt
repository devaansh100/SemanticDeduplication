Rare lizard dead, 5 found injured in Jaisalmer
Jaisalmer: A rare spiny-tailed lizard was found dead, while five of them were found injured near Pokhran field firing range of Jaisalmer district by the forest department. The skin of a Chinkara was also recovered from the spot. The lizard comes in the list of schedule II. Wildlife department ACF Balram Sharma said that the lizard species is on the verge of extinction and that they received information of poaching of spiny-tailed lizards and that some big poaching gang is active in the area. He said the forest department team immediately reached the spot, following which the poachers ran away. They recovered one dead & five severely injured lizards.
