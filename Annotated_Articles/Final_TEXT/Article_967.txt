Live Pangolin rescued and one arrested by STF
Cuttack,, 23/12/2020 ( Odisha Samachar )- Special Task Force(STF) of Crime Branch busted a Pangolin trade racket from Biribati area of Kandarpur Police Station and arrested one person . Arrested accused identified as Santosh Kumar Dalai of Gumutipur of Dhiasahi area of Jagatsinghpur District . STF rescued one Live Pangolin while arrested accused planning to sale it .
