
['Elephant tramples elderly man to death\nBaripada: In yet another case of human-animal conflict in the State, an elderly man was trampled death by a wild elephant at Dari village under Dudhiani range of Karanjia in Mayurbhanj district. The incident place when the man went to a nearby field to relieve himself this morning. The pachyderm came out of nowhere and before the man could act, the animal caught him up with its trunk and trampled him under the feet. On being informed, a team of forest department officials reached the spot and recovered the body for post mortem. The villagers are in a state of panic and have demanded the elephants be driven away before nightfall.']
