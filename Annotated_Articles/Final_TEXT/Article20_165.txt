
['Two arrested for poaching pangolin\nThe police arrested two persons on the charges of poaching pangolin, at Hiregadde. The arrested are Dinesh and Shivappa. Acting on a tip-off, the police raided and arrested the duo. Other accused Ramesh and Ravi are still at large. According to police, the arrested had killed a pangolin weighing 12 kg. A case has been registered against the accused under the Wildlife Protection Act.']
