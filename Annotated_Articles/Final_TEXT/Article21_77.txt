
['Uttarakhand: STF nabs man with leopard skin in Udham Singh Nagar\nRUDRAPUR: A joint team of Special Task Force STF ) of the Uttarakhand police and forest department on Wednesday arrested a man from Khatima town of Udham Singh Nagar (US Nagar) district, while he was smuggling a leopard hide. The accused has been identified as Veeru Prasad, a resident of Sampurna Nagar in Lakhimpur Khiri district of Uttar Pradesh.Chief of the STF Kumaon region, MP Singh said that a joint team was formed after they received a tip-off that a man was coming to US Nagar to sell leopard skin . "He was arrested from the city area and a hide of 7ft length and 4ft width was recovered from his possession. He told us that the big cat was killed last year using a wire snare in Chalthi forest range of Champawat district", said Inspector Singh.During interrogation, the accused revealed that he has been trafficking wild animals\' organs and skin for quite some time. "He has smuggled animals\' organs earlier to Uttar Pradesh and Delhi. We are interrogating him to know about the other people involved in poaching and smuggling," added Singh. The accused was booked under Wildlife Protection Act Notably, leopards enjoy the same level of protection as tigers under Schedule I of the Wildlife Protection Act, but they are regularly poached for their skins and other body parts. Leopard skins are used for home decoration or to make luxury carpets. A leopard skin costs 4 to 10 lakh rupees in international market.']
