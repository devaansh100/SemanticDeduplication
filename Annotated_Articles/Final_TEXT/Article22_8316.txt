
['Anandapur: Carcass of elephant found\nAnandapur: The carcass of an elephant was recovered at Kathakata forest of Hadgarh wildlife sanctuary. According to reports, the matter came to the fore after some locals spotted the carcass and informed forest officials. On being informed, the officials reached the spot and sent the carcass for an autopsy. The exact reason behind the death is yet to be ascertained. Following this, an investigation has been initiated in this regard.']
