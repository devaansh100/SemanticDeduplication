
['MM Hills wildlife officers seize deer meat, arrest two : Welcome to Mysooru News\nChamarajanagar: Forest personnel seized 6.5 kg meat of wild animal and arrested two accused in the case on Monday. Acting on a tip off, about deer meat being transported on a two-wheeler, Male Mahadeshwara Wildlife division officers led by ACF R Vanitha stopped the vehicle on Lokkanahalli stretch which connects to Gundal village in Kollegala sub-division. The accused Nagesh and Madevi were found transporting meat to Gundal village and were apprehended soon after the inspection. While another accused Somu ran away. MM Hills wildlife division DCF V Yedukondalu has appreciated the personnel in checking wildlife crime. -Team Mysoorunews ']
