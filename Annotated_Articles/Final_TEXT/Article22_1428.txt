
['Mumbai: Truck driver arrested for running over stray dog\nA 45-year-old truck driver from Bengaluru has been arrested and his vehicle seized for allegedly running over a stray dog in Andheri (west) in the early hours of Monday, said the police. The incident took place around 1.45 am near the Bharat Petroleum pump in the Kapaswadi area of Andheri (west), said the police.
