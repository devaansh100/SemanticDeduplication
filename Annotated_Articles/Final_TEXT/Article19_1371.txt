
['Leopard rescued from residential building near Vijay Nagar in Marol\nMUMBAI: Following hours of struggle, forest officials managed to rescue a leopard from a residential society in Mumbai on Monday.Earlier, a leopard was spotted by residents at Woodland Crest residential society near Vijay Nagar in Marol on Monday morning. The big cat was first spotted sleeping under a vehicle in the locality. The area is barely 100 mtrs away from the boundary wall of Aarey Milk Colony Two teams of forest officials including, Santosh Kank and honorary wildlife warden Pawan Sharma, were rushed to the spot.A resident of Woodland Crest tower, Pramod S, said, "I saw the leopard in the compound. It later went underneath my white-coloured Innova car which is parked in the complex."']
