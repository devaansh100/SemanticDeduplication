Palghar: Gutkha worth over Rs 11 lakh was seized from Palghar district in Maharashtra and two persons were arrested in this connection, police said on Tuesday. Also Read: Gutkha worth Rs 51.83 lakh seized in Maharashtra, 3 arrested A police team intercepted a tempo on the Mumbai- Ahmedabad highway near Amboli village here on Monday and found gutkha worth Rs 11.96 lakh being carried in the vehicle, police spokesperson Hemant Katkar said. The tempo driver and cleaner, both hailing from Panvel in neighbouring Raigad district, were arrested following the seizure and booked under relevant sections of the Indian Penal Code and the Food Safety and Standards Act, he said.
