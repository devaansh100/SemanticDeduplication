
["Five-year-old girl trampled to death by elephant in Kerala's Thrissur\nA five-year-old girl was trampled to death by a wild elephant in Kerala's Thrissur district. Two others were injured in the elephant attack. The girl succumbed to her injuries before reaching the hospital. By Rickson Oommen: A five-year-old girl was trampled to death by an elephant in Kerala's Thrissur district. The wild tusker ventured into a residential area in Puthenchira, killing the girl and injuring two others. On Monday, five-year-old Agnimiya and her family spotted the wild elephant close to their home. The family ran but the girl was attacked by the elephant. Her father Nikhil and another relative, Jayan, tried to rescue her and suffered injuries. The girl was trampled to death. advertisement All three were taken to a private hospital in Chalakkudy. Agnimiya succumbed to her injuries before reaching the hospital.
