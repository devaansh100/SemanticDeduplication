(Representational Image) As many as five people were injured after being attacked by a leopard which had entered the premises of an under construction building in the residential area in Mundhwa suburb of Pune on Monday morning. Officials from the forest department, police and staff from local zoo with the help of fire brigade personnel captured the leopard after a 2 two-hour long operation. As per the information given by local police, a leopard was spotted in Keshavnagar area in Mundhwa around 6.30 am. Traps were laid to capture the animal. (Express Photo) Traps were laid to capture the animal. (Express Photo) As per the initial reports, an elderly woman and man were attacked by the animal after which the police were informed. Senior inspector Anil Patrudkar of Mundhwa police station said, “Teams from forest department and fire brigade and those from the local zoo were called to trap the animal which after injuring some persons hid inside the building. Traps were laid to catch the animal and police teams were deployed for crowd control. The leopard was caught after an hour-long operation.” Range forest officer for Pune, VJ Gaikwad said, “A forest guard, policeman and staffer from zoo sustained injuries as the animal was being trapped. Earlier it has attacked an elderly woman and a man.
