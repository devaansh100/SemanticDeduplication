Poacher With Leopard Skin Held In Kandhamal
Phulbani: Forest officials on Thursday arrested a poacher and seized a leopard skin along with its nails, bones, and other items from his possession in Sudrukumpa range under Phulbani Forest Division in Kandhamal district. Reportedly, the accused identified as Kabi Chandra Kanhar of Kutibari village had killed a leopard in the nearby forest recently.
