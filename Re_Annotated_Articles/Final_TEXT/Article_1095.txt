2 poachers arrested with leopard skin in Dehradun
DEHRADUN: Dehradun police on Wednesday arrested two members of inter-state wildlife poaching and smuggling gang and seized one leopard skin from them.Acting on a tip-off provided by wildlife crime control bureau, a team of Dehradun police nabbed the smugglers, identified as Dharam Singh and Chandar Singh Chauhan , both residents of Dehradun. According to senior superintendent of police Arun Mohan Joshi , “Both were arrested from Anaarwala area of the city with a 8-ft-long leopard skin. Both confessed of killing the leopard near Chakrata last month. They said that they brought the skin to Dehradun for sale.”
