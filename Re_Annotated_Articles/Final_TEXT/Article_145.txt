Three try to smuggle sand boa in Tiruvallur, arrested
Picture used for representational purpose only CHENNAI: Three people were caught on Thursday night trying to smuggle a red sand boa for sale near Tiruvallur. Red sand boa is a snake protected under the Wildlife Protection Act. Forest department officials booked them under ScheduleIVof theIndian Wildlife Protection Act,1972. Based on a tip-off, police rounded up Udhayakumar, 28, Thangamani, 42, and Ponniyan, 50, and found they had trapped the sand boa in a cloth bag. The snake was about 4 feet long and weighed 1kg. Police handed over the trio and the reptile to forest department officials. Forest officials said the triotoldthem thatthey had procured the snake from a friend.
