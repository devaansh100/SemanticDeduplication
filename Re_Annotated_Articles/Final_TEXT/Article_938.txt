One held for killing antelope in Nashik
One held for killing antelope in Nashik Nashik, Aug 13 (UNI) A Forest department team has arrested a man for allegedly killing a male antelope at Dongargaon Shivar in Yeola taluka of the district. Acting on a tip off that a gang of 4 to 5 men was hunting an antelope by stoning it to death in a field at Manje Dongaron Shivar in Yeola taluka on Tuesday, the Forest department team rushed to the spot and detected one of the suspects while his other accomplices managed to flee, sources said. The suspect, Madhukar Shivaji Pawar, a resident of Bilwani in Vaijapur taluka, was arrested.
