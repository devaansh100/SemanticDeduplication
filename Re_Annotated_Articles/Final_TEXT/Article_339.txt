Pangolin skins recovered, two persons detained in Guwahati
STAFF REPORTER  GUWAHATI: A joint operation of the Wildlife Crime Control Bureau and Forest officials led to the recovery of 5 kg of pangolin skins from a car (AS-01-AF 4844), and also detained two persons travelling in the car. The two detained persons are Mohammed Shah Nawaz (from Nagaon) and Faizul Hoque (from Karimganj). Investigation is on.
