Troops seize illegally transported timber
Troops seize illegally transported timber Imphal, Dec 10 (UNI) Assam Rifles intercepted a huge consignment of timber costing worth crores being ferried without legal documents close to Angbreshu village in Chandel district today. Based on specific intelligence input regarding the movement of illicit timber along NH - 102, a deliberate operation was launched by the troops of Assam Rifles and seized four large capacity vehicles carrying wooden logs without legal documents worth crores. The recovered logs of timber have been handed over to Forest Department Chandel for further legal action.
