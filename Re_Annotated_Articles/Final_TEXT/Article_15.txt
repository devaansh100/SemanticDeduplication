Based on an intelligence input regarding illegal possession & trade of Pangolin scales by some unscrupulous persons in Odisha, Joint team of #WCCB and Odisha Forest Department conducted a joint operation on 24-07-2021 and seized 15.2 kgs. of #Pangolin #scales and arrested two persons at Dhenkanal, Odisha.
