Three held with skin, bones of leopard in Dindori district
Three held with skin, bones of leopard in Dindori district   Staff Reporter : According to WCCB Jabalpur, during a special campaign against poaching and illegal trade of wildlife under the direction of In-charge officer of STF (Wildlife), Madhya Pradesh and Abhijit Roy Choudhary, Regional Deputy Director, WCCB Jabalpur, they received a tip-off that some persons were trying to sell leopard’s skin and bones. Taking prompt action on information, a combined team of WCCB Jabalpur, STF Jabalpur and Achanakmar Tiger Reserve, Chhattisgarh started investigation and fielded a buyer for the leopard’s skin.
